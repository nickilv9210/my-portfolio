About Me

My professional focus lies at the intersection of data analytics and automation, turning complex datasets into clear, actionable solutions that drive business decisions.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, traveling and exploring new places, and studying history and genealogy. Projects

I have developed automation tools to streamline complex state incentive programs, including Missouri Works and Georgia Job Tax Credit (JTC). These programs require detailed and recurring reporting, which I translated into efficient workflows using R and Excel.

To make these tools accessible to non-technical users, I packaged the code into standalone executable (.exe) applications. This allows users to run the reporting and analysis without needing to install any software or have coding experience, significantly improving efficiency and consistency across projects.
